    Overview

    Enchanter is a 3D adventure game built on the Endstar platform where players embark on a quest to fix a magical painting and break a kingdom's curse. The game combines exploration, puzzle-solving, and narrative storytelling within an immersive fantasy world. The project earned the People's Choice Award for its compelling art direction, engaging gameplay mechanics, and the seamless way it weaves player agency into a rich storyline.
