Overview

The Agriculture and National Security Website is a specialized CMS interface that bridges the gap between agricultural science and national defense policy. Designed to serve both researchers and decision-makers, the platform organizes complex interdisciplinary content into an accessible structure. The interface facilitates knowledge sharing across two traditionally siloed domains, helping stakeholders understand how food systems and national security intersect.
