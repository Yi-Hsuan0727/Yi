About me

I am a UX/UI designer focused on product thinking, interface craft, and practical systems. I enjoy turning complex problems into clear, useful digital experiences rooted in real user stories.

Originally from Taiwan, I moved to Arizona in August 2024 to pursue my M.S. in UX/HCI at Arizona State University, graduating in May 2026. I speak fluent Mandarin and am learning Japanese and German.

I've designed across web + mobile, shipped front-end work, and love collaborating closely with engineers. I'm also a hackathon award winner (2nd Place, RoseHack 2025) and I care deeply about community—through volunteering, serving as a Student Adobe Ambassador at ASU, and my work with Toastmasters.

Outside of work, I recharge through animation, design and art, travel, Minecraft, and saying yes to new things.

That's me!
