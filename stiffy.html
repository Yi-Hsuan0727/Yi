Stiffy Wanderers is an interactive mobile game where players guide a rock character through a series of environmental missions based on real-time location data. The game encourages outdoor exploration by tying in-game progress to physical movement and geographic discovery. By blending location-based gameplay with environmental storytelling, Stiffy Wanderers motivates players to engage with the world around them while completing creative challenges.
