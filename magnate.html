Multilingual Website

Supporting Three Language Versions

The website's content is available in two languages, namely traditional Chinese, Japanese and English. To create a multilingual website, the interface must be able to accommodate the characteristics of both languages. For example, English phrases are often longer than Chinese ones. Therefore, the website should allocate more space to accommodate lengthy English phrases. As a result, it is necessary to double-check the website under different language circumstances.
