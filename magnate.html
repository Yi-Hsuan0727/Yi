Overview

Magnate Technology commissioned our team to redesign and build its official website to establish a stronger digital presence. The project focused on presenting aerospace manufacturing capabilities with a clear information hierarchy and a consistent visual identity.

Design Focus

The site needed to support multilingual content and remain visually stable across Traditional Chinese, Japanese, and English layouts. We designed flexible content modules and responsive behavior to keep readability strong under different text lengths.

  • Multilingual-ready interface structure.
  • Responsive web layout with reusable UI sections.
  • Clear communication of company capabilities and services.

Outcome

The delivered website improved brand consistency, clarified technical content for visitors, and provided a scalable foundation for future updates.
