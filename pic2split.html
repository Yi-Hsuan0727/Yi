Overview

An AI-powered, web-first receipt splitter that makes group dining effortless

Pic2Split is an AI-powered web service designed to eliminate the friction of group dining and shared expenses. Using a "Web-First" approach, we created a tool that requires no app download, allowing users to scan a receipt and split complex bills fairly in seconds.

As the Product Designer, I led the end-to-end design process, from identifying critical failures in existing market solutions to delivering a high-fidelity, 23-screen design system that prioritizes speed and calculation accuracy.

01 — Problem Statement

Calculating a North American restaurant bill is manual, error-prone, and trust-destroying

Calculating a North American restaurant bill with subtotal, tax, and tip is often a manual and error-prone task. Our research indicated that users found current tools to be either too simplistic or technically unreliable.

Calculation Fatigue

Manual itemization often leads to a $0.01–$0.05 discrepancy, causing frustration.

Low OCR Trust

Users expect AI to work perfectly but are often met with "terrible" text extraction that requires more work to fix than manual entry.

Tax & Tip Confusion

Most apps do not clearly distinguish between percentage-based and fixed-dollar additions.

02 — Project Goals

Two aligned goals guided every scope and design decision

Research findings shaped a focused set of design goals — defining what success looked like not just for the final product, but for every sprint and screen decision. The user and business goals reinforced each other: faster meant less friction, and less friction meant better retention.

User Goal

Create a fast, intuitive way to scan a receipt, itemize it, assign items to people, and share the results instantly.

Business / Product Goal

Deliver a functional web-first MVP that keeps the average session completion under 2 minutes.

04 — Key Solutions

Smart assignment, transparent math, and instant web sharing

Five core features work together to eliminate every friction point — from login to the final shared link.

Solution 01

Frictionless Google Authentication

One-tap login stores your splitting history safely without tedious onboarding forms.

Solution 02

Instant OCR Side-by-Side Review

Users snap or upload a photo, and the AI extracts items, prices, tax, and tip instantly. The UI places this data alongside the receipt photo for quick confirmation.

Solution 03

Tap-to-Split Multi-Selection

Tap an item, select the clean text tags showing the names of the members who shared it, and watch the app divide the cost dynamically and equally.

Solution 04

Proportional Tax & Tip Engine

The backend accurately distributes tax and tip proportionally based only on what each individual actually consumed.

Solution 05

Web-First Real-Time Sharing

Generates a clean, transparent web summary link or a QR code. Friends can view exactly what they owe in a browser without downloading an app.

Web link

A clean browser page showing each person's total.

QR code

Scan at the table — no typing a URL.

05 — Reflection

Technology alone doesn't solve user problems — trust and transparency do

Building an AI-driven product taught me that technology alone doesn't solve user problems — trust and transparency do. By evaluating our competitors, we learned that when AI isn't 100% transparent, users feel out of control. Designing fallback mechanisms, like manual editing and side-by-side reviews, built that trust.

06 — Summary & Impact

We eliminated the friction of manual bill splitting, especially for complex U.S. receipts with tax and tip. We removed the barrier to entry by making it a web-first platform with seamless Google login.

We successfully cut down the bill-splitting process from 5–10 minutes of manual math to just seconds. We delivered a functional MVP combining React, PaddleOCR, and Gemini-2.5.
